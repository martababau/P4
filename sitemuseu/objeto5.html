Washer

The Washer is a versatile tool designed for scrubbing. Made from synthetic materials and it's spikes, it has a structure that allows it to brush to clean rough and abrasive components. Ideal for cleaning dishes, countertops, and various surfaces, the Washer was an essential household item that effectively helped in removing dirt and grime, making cleaning tasks easier and more efficient.

HOW TO USE-WASHER

STEP 1: You hold the Washer by his handle;

STEP 2: You scrub the dirt of the object you want to clean;

STEP 3: You clean the Washer using water;
