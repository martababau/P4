Condimenter

This object was used as a kitchen utensil. It consists of a small glass bottle that screws onto a metal tube with a small hole, connected to a kind of pump that disperses the product gently throughout the food when squeezed. It can be used with different types of sauces and condiments, as long as they have a liquid texture, so that it can be sprayed well over the meal. It was a very useful utensil in the kitchen of our ancestors.
