Glarer

This object was designed to provide illumination to areas. It features a plastic handle encased in metal wires, designed to hold a small candle inside. The handle includes a hole, allowing it to be tied to various locations and hang in a room without special attachments. Despite its rudimentary design, it served its purpose well, as humans had no other means of lighting up spaces back then.

HOW TO USE-GLARER

STEP 1: You light a candle using fire;

STEP 2: You insert the candle inside the metal wire's cage;

STEP 3: You hold the Glarer inserting the hole on the handle into a secure spot;

STEP 4: You now, safely, avoid the candle's flame to disappear;
