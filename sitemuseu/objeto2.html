Strokers

Strokers were an artistic tool highly valued by our ancestors. They are made up of some metals, having a very shiny appearance and each one of these objects has a unique shape, which allows us to create different patterns and finishes with the use of paint. To use it, you have to hold each stroker by the flat end with a hole and the shape of the other end has a pattern to create art; We can see the results of strokers in various abstract archaic works.

HOW TO USE-STROKERS

Step 1: Prepare your strokers, paper and some paint. you will also need something to hold the paint. you can use as many colors as you want, and as many strokers as you want, since all of them have different shapes.

Step 2: Grab you strokers by their handle and dip the bottom of the stroker in the paint.

Step 3: Now you can start your creative work! start by creating shapes and strokes with the strokers by rubbing them on the paper. You can take advantage of the different colors and shapes of the strokers.
