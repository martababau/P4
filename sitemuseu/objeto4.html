Swappers

On display we also have a rare form of ancient trade, swappers. They were discovered inside a glass jar; they have a round shape and are composed by materials such as plastic and ceramic; After some research we discovered that these objects were an important currency for our ancestors and varied in size, color and design, thus their market value depended on these factors; Although we have some of these in our exhibit, we found more types of swappers that are yet to be analyzed.
